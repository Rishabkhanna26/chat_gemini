- Fees are as per signed Proposal/SOW.
- All invoices are payable within `[7/15]` days from invoice date.

- Responsibility for accuracy and legality of client-supplied content/data.

## 10. Warranty
- Provider warrants services will be performed professionally and materially as per SOW.
- Bug-fix warranty period for accepted deliverables: `[30]` days from go-live, limited to defects within approved scope.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

- Provider's total cumulative liability under this Agreement is capped at fees actually paid by Client in the preceding `[3]` months.

## 12. Third-Party Services

- For WhatsApp/cloud/SMS/email APIs, usage and compliance obligations are shared with Client as account owner.
